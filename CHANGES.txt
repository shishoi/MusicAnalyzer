Duplicate Detection Logic Updates - Summary

Changes made to audio_analyzer.py:
1. Added 'year' and 'bpm' fields to metadata extraction
2. Added download site words to VERSION_KEYWORDS (youtube, spotidownloader, etc.)
3. Enhanced similarity calculation with:
   - Duration penalty: Exact match required (>2 sec difference = major penalty)
   - Year mismatch penalty: Punishes different years
   - BPM mismatch penalty: Punishes BPM differences >1
   - Title similarity: NOW MORE IMPORTANT than filename (2.0x weight)
   - Filename similarity: Reduced importance (0.8x weight)
4. Improved normalization: Removes punctuation, version words, download site words
